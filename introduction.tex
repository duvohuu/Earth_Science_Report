    \section*{Purpose of Conducting a Field Report}

    The main purpose of preparing a field report is to systematically document observations, measurements, and analyses made directly at a study site. It serves several key objectives:

    \begin{enumerate}
        \item \textbf{Recording empirical data:} Collect accurate and detailed information about geological features, rock types, structures, and environmental conditions.
        
        \item \textbf{Supporting scientific analysis:} Provide evidence for interpreting geological processes, history, and relationships between rock formations.
        
        \item \textbf{Enhancing understanding:} Develop practical skills in field observation, mapping, and data interpretation.
        
        \item \textbf{Facilitating communication:} Share findings clearly with peers, instructors, or researchers, allowing others to verify results and build upon them.
        
        \item \textbf{Guiding future studies:} Identify patterns, anomalies, or areas of interest for further investigation and research.
    \end{enumerate}

    In summary, a field report ensures that observations made in situ are systematically recorded, analyzed, and communicated, forming a foundation for scientific study and decision-making in earth sciences.

    \section*{Natural, Economic, and Social Characteristics of the Study Area}

    \subsection*{Natural Characteristics}

    \begin{enumerate}
        \item \textbf{Geographic location:}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item Bà Rịa – Vũng Tàu is located in the Southeast region of Vietnam, bordering Đồng Nai, Bình Thuận provinces, and Ho Chi Minh City. To the southeast, it faces the East Sea.
        \end{itemize}
        
        \item \textbf{Topography:}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item The area has diverse terrain with a long coastline, hills, mountains, and plains. The highest mountain is Núi Chúa at 503 m. Vũng Tàu is famous for its beautiful beaches, such as Bãi Trước and Bãi Sau.
        \end{itemize}
        
        \item \textbf{Climate:}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item Tropical monsoon climate with two distinct seasons: the rainy season from May to October and the dry season from November to April. The average temperature is about 27°C.
        \end{itemize}
        
        \item \textbf{Hydrology:}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item The river system is quite abundant, notably including the Dinh River, Ray River, and a network of canals connected to the sea.
        \end{itemize}
    \end{enumerate}

    \subsection*{Economic Characteristics}

    \begin{enumerate}
        \item \textbf{Industry:}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item Bà Rịa – Vũng Tàu is an important industrial center, particularly in oil and gas, chemicals, and seafood processing. Major industrial zones include Phú Mỹ and Cái Mép – Thị Vải.
        \end{itemize}
        
        \item \textbf{Tourism:}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item Tourism is a key economic sector, with many attractive destinations such as Bãi Sau, Hồ Cốc, Long Hải, and Côn Đảo. The province attracts millions of domestic and international tourists each year.
        \end{itemize}
        
        \item \textbf{Agriculture:}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item Agriculture is developed with crops such as rice, rubber, pepper, and fruit trees. Aquaculture, especially cage fish and shrimp farming, also makes a significant contribution to the local economy.
        \end{itemize}
        
        \item \textbf{Transportation:}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item The transportation system is well-developed, featuring important seaports like Cái Mép – Thị Vải Port and Vũng Tàu Port, along with a road network connecting neighboring provinces and Ho Chi Minh City.
        \end{itemize}
    \end{enumerate}

    \subsection*{Social Characteristics}

    \begin{enumerate}
        \item \textbf{Population:}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item The population of Bà Rịa – Vũng Tàu is approximately 1.1 million people, with diverse ethnicities and cultures. The main ethnic groups include Kinh, Hoa, Khmer, and Chăm.
        \end{itemize}
        
        \item \textbf{Education:}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item The education system is strongly invested in, with many schools, training centers, and universities such as Bà Rịa – Vũng Tàu University. The quality of education is steadily improving.
        \end{itemize}
        
        \item \textbf{Healthcare:}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item The healthcare system is well-developed, with numerous hospitals, medical centers, and clinics. Bà Rịa Hospital and Lê Lợi Hospital are important healthcare facilities.
        \end{itemize}
        
        \item \textbf{Culture:}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item Bà Rịa – Vũng Tàu has many traditional festivals and historical sites, such as the Temple of Saint Trần Hưng Đạo, Long Sơn Great House, and various folk festivals. Maritime culture is also an important part of community life.
        \end{itemize}
        
        \item \textbf{Society:}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item The quality of life for residents has been steadily improving, with increasingly adequate and modern public services and social amenities. The poverty rate has decreased significantly, and people's living standards continue to rise.
        \end{itemize}
    \end{enumerate}
